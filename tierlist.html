Description of Tier Criteria

  • Tier 0: A "meta" unit that excels at their role. Provides enormous benefit in endgame content, and requires minimal outside investment.
  • Tier 1: A considerably strong unit that may not be best in class at their role, but are very competitive with Tier 0 units.
  • Tier 2: Inferior to units in Tier 1, but still very strong at their role, and can still easily be used to clear endgame content. Will require some investment, but can still perform very well.
  • Tier 3: Units that require considerable investment to perform well in their roles, but are still usable to clear endgame content. They have less redeemable features and are harder to build than Tier 2 units
  • Tier 4: Units that are simply disadvantaged from their base kit and SC saves, and usually have trouble performing even with heavy investment.
